Colosseum

The Colosseum, located in Rome, Italy, is one of the most iconic amphitheaters of the ancient world. Built in 70-80 AD, it hosted gladiator battles, public spectacles, and theatrical performances. Despite centuries of wear, it remains a symbol of Roman engineering and history.

Trevi Fountain

The Trevi Fountain is a masterpiece of Baroque architecture, located in Rome, Italy. Built in the 18th century, it is famous for its stunning sculptures and the tradition of tossing a coin into its waters for good luck. It attracts millions of visitors every year as a symbol of beauty and wishes.
